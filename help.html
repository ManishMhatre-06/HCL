Help & Support

Welcome to the HCL Help Center. Whether you are getting started with the Human Centric Language or exploring advanced features, this section is designed to guide you through the available resources and support channels.

We recommend using the following guides based on your needs:

  • Language Guide →
    If you need assistance with understanding HCL syntax, writing programs, or learning how natural language instructions are interpreted and executed, refer to the Language Guide. This section covers installation, fundamentals, and practical usage.
  • Compiler Guide →
    For help related to the HCL Compiler, including setup, execution workflow, and integration with the AI-assisted system, visit the Compiler Guide.
  • Contact Support →
    If your issue is not covered in the documentation or you require further assistance, feel free to reach out to us at humancentricpl@gmail.com. Our team will review your query and respond within 24 hours.

Our goal is to ensure a smooth and intuitive experience while using HCL. If you encounter any issues, suggestions, or unexpected behavior, we encourage you to contact us — your feedback helps improve the system.
